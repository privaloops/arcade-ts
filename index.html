The first browser-based CPS1 ROM studio. Capture sprites and backgrounds, edit in Aseprite, export MAME-compatible ROMs.

MAME-compatible export

From ROM to Aseprite and back

Three steps. No toolchain. No install.

1

Capture

Play the game — ROMstudio auto-captures every sprite pose and scroll tile. Click REC, play, stop.

2

Edit in Aseprite

Export .aseprite files with embedded ROM metadata. Indexed 8bpp, 16-color CPS1 palettes, tilemaps — all preserved.

3

Import & Play

Import back — tiles patched into GFX ROM instantly. See your edits in the running game. Export a MAME-compatible ZIP.

A complete arcade studio

X-Ray Mode

Separate the 4 layers in 3D. See how the CPS-A/CPS-B ASICs compose each frame — scroll planes, sprites, priority, palette — all in real time.

Audio Studio

8 FM channels (YM2151), 4 ADPCM voices (OKI), QSound surround — browse every sound, mute/solo channels, replace samples by drag-and-drop. Record from your mic.

MAME-Compatible Export

Export your modded ROM as a standard ZIP. Sprites, tiles, palettes, samples — everything included. Runs in MAME, FBNeo, or any compatible emulator. No vendor lock-in.

Built from scratch. Every byte.

No emulation library. TypeScript + WebGL2 + WASM.
