# E. Honda — SF2 Hyper Fighting

Typical early-stage CPU opponent. Bulky sumo with strong punish on sloppy
zoning. Usually the first fight in arcade order.

## Specials (the AI uses all of these)

- **Hundred Hand Slap (Hyakuretsu Harite)**: mash Punch rapidly. Fast multi-hit at close range. AI spams this in close-range pressure.
- **Sumo Headbutt (Koshi no Kage)**: hold-back charge, then forward + punch. Flying headbutt crossing the screen. Comes out straight and punishable on whiff.
- **Sumo Splash (Super Zutsuki)**: hold-down charge, then up + kick. Jumping splash, doubles as an anti-air.
- **Oicho Throw**: command grab at close range. Avoid being grounded within grab distance.

## AI behavior patterns

| Pattern | Trigger | Counter |
|---------|---------|---------|
| Sumo Headbutt punish | Ryu throws a fireball at mid-to-long range | Don't zone lazily — vary timing or walk forward |
| Hundred Hand Slap spam | Ryu stays grounded in close range | Jump back / neutral jump — HHS has no anti-air |
| Sumo Splash anti-air | Ryu jumps in from mid range | Anti-air HIM first with Shoryuken, or empty-jump bait |
| Walk-in pressure | HP lead at mid range | Poke with c.MK to keep Honda honest |
| Desperation jumps | Honda under 20% HP | Read the jump, anti-air |
| Oicho grab | Touching range, both grounded | Jump / back-dash / quick button |

## Counter strategy for Ryu

- **Patient zoning but vary timing** — predictable Hadoukens eat headbutts for free.
- **Jump back** on close-range pressure to escape HHS, then land with j.HK.
- **Anti-air with Shoryuken** when Honda tries Sumo Splash.
- **Punish whiffed headbutt** — massive recovery, free 40%+ combo.
- **Never get thrown near the corner** — Oicho loops are brutal.
